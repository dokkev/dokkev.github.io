Dong Ho Kang

Robotics PhD Student at the University of Texas at Austin

dongho@utexas.edu

My name is Dong Ho Kang (DK), and I am a PhD student investiages design optimization of humanoid robots at Human Centered Robotics Lab in the University of Texas at Austin. I focus on bridging the gap between skill-level manipulation and robot hardware by integrating robot morphology, mechanism, and control as a single, coupled design problem.
